What We Do

One call for almost
any home repair.

No job is too small. Whether it's a quick fix or an ongoing maintenance plan, Mike shows up on time, does the work right, and leaves your home better than he found it.

General Repairs

Doors that stick, cabinets off their hinges, drywall dings — the everyday fixes that pile up. Mike handles them efficiently and affordably.

HVAC Services

Filter replacements, unit tune-ups, vent cleaning, and basic HVAC maintenance to keep your system running efficiently all year.

Plumbing Fixes

Leaky faucets, running toilets, slow drains, and minor pipe repairs. Fast, clean work that saves you from bigger problems down the road.

Electrical Work

Outlet replacements, light fixture installs, switch repairs, and ceiling fan installation. Safe, code-compliant work by a licensed pro.

Painting & Drywall

Interior painting, drywall patching, texture matching, and trim work. Clean lines, no mess left behind, and colors that last.

Seasonal Maintenance

Gutter cleaning, caulking, weatherstripping, and pre-winter prep. Stay ahead of Ohio's weather before it becomes a costly problem.

About Mike

A neighbor you can
actually trust.

Mike has been serving Northeast Ohio homeowners for over two decades. He grew up in the area, raised his family here, and built his business entirely on word-of-mouth — which tells you everything you need to know.

There are no subcontractors, no bait-and-switch quotes, and no disappearing acts. When you call Mike, Mike shows up. He'll walk through the job with you, give you a straight price, and get it done right.

Whether it's a weekend honey-do list or something that's been nagging at you for months, Mike is the kind of handyman your grandparents had — dependable, skilled, and genuinely invested in doing good work for the community he calls home.
